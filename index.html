UK Accidents in December 2010

Visualisations By Hakimi Aiman

Number of Casualties in UK

The number of casualties at some location can be seen really high. For instance at location of longitude of 1.266279 and latitude of 52.687243 had 42 total of casualties while some location like longitude of -2.913953 and -2.039817 and latitude of 54.389869 and 51.929856 respectively had only total of casualties of 1.

Number of Casualties Based on Casualty Class and Age Categories

In the case of casualty class of being driver or rider, it can be clearly seen that most of the reported cases are in the range of 26 to 35 years old. Also number casualties that involving passenger as the main factor contributing to the accidents are significantly low compare to other casualty classes such driver or rider and pedestrian. Same goes to the age that in range of below 5 years old, the number of casualties are very low.

Number of Casualties and Total Vehicles Involved per Month

The size of casualties are much similar on each month. There will be a day where reported cases of number of casualties are really high as it can be seen in September and December where the total number of casualties or number of times accidents happened been reported are 42 and 43 respectively on that month. However number of vehicles involved are very low, thus road type or weather condition might be the most contributed factor causing the accidents.
